2001

Grainger and Keeler probably met at a Sonic Youth concert, but sometimes claim to have met in prison, on a pirate ship, or in a gay bar. Journalists are still piecing together the clues, but we'll get back to you on that one.

2002

Heads Up, Death From Above's first release, is unleashed on the world.

2004

The action packed follow up record You're a Woman, I'm a Machine is released.

Death From Above becomes Death From Above 1979 for a bit, here's the lads with more on that:

FUCK DFA RECORDS FUCK JAMES MURPHY WE DECLARE JIHAD ON THEM HOLY WAR ENDING IN THIER [sic] DEATH AND DISMEMBERMENT... james murphy is a selfish piece of fuck that will burn in the flames of a specially dedicated rock and roll jihad. if i had the resources i would fly a plane into his skull.

2006

The band breaks up for a bit, can't be class 24/7.

2011

Back in it baby, couple of new shows to boot.

2014

The Physical World is unleashed upon the...physical world.

The band releases the documentary Life After Death from Above 1979 on the breakup and makeup of the lads.

2017

Outrage! is Now is Now!

Also, the band sees what happens if you mess around with legal precedents. Nothing, apparently, so they drop the 1979 from here on.
